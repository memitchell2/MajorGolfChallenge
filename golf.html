Best Golf Balls

This is my official golf ball ranking. These are my criteria:
1. Price, as a high handicapper you have to love budget golf balls
2. Performance, the best ball is a ball that goes far
3. Style, look good play good

Number 1:
Kirkland Signature, it is hard to find any other golf ball that is cheaper let alone performs better. A excellent golf ball that may be the only reason that I will get a "Costco Membership", and oh did I mention that their old school design is excellent? Well it is, and this is the best golf ball on the market.

B-52 Bomber, a bit of an underdog pick but I love this golf balls
